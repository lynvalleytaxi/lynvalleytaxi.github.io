Lyn Valley Taxi

Established in 2008, Lyn Valley Taxi is a local taxi service based in Lynton, offering a friendly and reliable service for all types of journeys and tours around Exmoor.

Available 7am - 11pm.

All other times available by prior arrangement.


Services we offer

  • Walking route drop-offs
    If walking is your passion, we offer a drop-off or collection service either at the start or finish of your walk. Popular local walks include Porlock, Combe Martin, Hunter's Inn, Brendon, County Gate, Rockford, and many others around Exmoor.
  • Railway station transfers
    Collection and drop-off available for Taunton, Tiverton Parkway, and Barnstaple, 24/7 by prior arrangement. The National Express also departs from Barnstaple.
  • Airport transfers
    Collection and drop-off available for Bristol International Airport and Exeter International Airport, 24/7 by prior arrangement.
  • Luggage transfers
    Lynton, Lynmouth, Porlock, Combe Martin and Simonsbath areas covered.
  • Appointments
    We are available for drop-off and collection from hospital and health appointments in Barnstaple, Exeter and Taunton.

Well behaved dogs welcome.
